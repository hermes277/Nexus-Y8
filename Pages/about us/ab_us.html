Aim

The aim of a job seeking website is to provide a comprehensive platform for
job seekers and employers to connect with each other. The website should have a user-friendly
interface that allows job seekers to easily search and apply for job opportunities based on their skills and qualifications.
It should also offer various tools and resources such as resume builders, interview preparation guides, and career advice to help job seekers increase their chances of success. Additionally, the website should aim to provide employers with
access to a diverse pool of qualified candidates and streamline the recruitment process. Overall, the goal of
a job seeking website is to facilitate the job search process and help individuals
find meaningful employment opportunities.

Purpose

Looking for a job can be a daunting and time-consuming task for both job seekers and employers.
Our job seeking website offers a comprehensive solution to help bridge the gap between job seekers and employers. For
job seekers, our platform provides a user-friendly interface that allows them to easily search and apply for job opportunities.
They can also upload their resume and receive alerts when a new job that matches their skills and experience becomes available. For employers, our website offers a pool of highly qualified candidates, making the recruitment process more efficient and cost-effective. Employers can post job openings, search resumes, and communicate with potential candidates all in one place.
With our job seeking website, we strive to make the job search and hiring process simpler and more efficient
for both job seekers and employers.
